Privacy-First Learning

Your learning progress is stored in your own Solid Pod - a personal data storage space that you control completely. We never see your data, and you can delete it anytime.

Why Solid Pods?

  • • You own your data, not us
  • • End-to-end encryption
  • • No corporate surveillance
  • • Compatible with activist privacy needs
